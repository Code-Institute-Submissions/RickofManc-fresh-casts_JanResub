Why this site exists.


I love podcasts. I assume you love podcasts. The problem I found is discoverability of new podcasts. The chances are you unearth new podcasts from a friend, Google or a chart within a listening app. These sites use a ranking system that ensures top shows get the most plays> This keeps them at the top of the pile, and it all becomes a bit stale.


Let's share the freshest podcasts together.


Here you can explore what's fresh through five key categories, and with a user account you can become a contributor.
Show your appreciation of a post with a like, or comment to join in a discussion and provide your view. You can also contribute to content by adding a blog post and sharing what's fresh to you.
