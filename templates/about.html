Why this site exists.


I love podcast. I assume you love podcasts. The problem I found is discoverability of new podcasts. The chances are you unearth new podcasts from a friend or a chart via your smartphone or search engine provider. These sites use a ranking system that is ensures the top shows get the most plays, and this keep them at the top, and it all becomes a bit stale.


Let's share the freshest podcasts together.


Here you can explore what's fresh through five key categories, and with a user account you can become a contributor.
Show your appreciation of a post with a like, or comment to join in and provide your view. You can also contribute to the content by creating your own post and sharing what's fresh to you.
